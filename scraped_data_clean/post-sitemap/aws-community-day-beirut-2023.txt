AWS Community Day Beirut 2023 About The Event: The AWS Community Day is a complimentary and inclusive event that convenes AWS enthusiasts, professionals, and businesses to engage in knowledge sharing, acquire insights on the latest AWS technologies, and foster connections with industry peers. The event uniquely emphasizes startups, thereby establishing a novel paradigm for such gatherings within the region. During the AWS Community Day, we have scheduled over 6 engaging speakers, a discussion panel, and 3 workshops that will run sequentially but in parallel with the main event. If you’re interested in participating in the workshops and learning from them, don’t forget to bring your laptop along.
