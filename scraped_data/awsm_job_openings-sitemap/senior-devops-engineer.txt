 Senior DevOps Engineer
 Careers
 About Digico Solutions:
 Digico Solutions, an esteemed AWS APN Advanced Consulting Partner with headquarters in Abu Dhabi and branches across Dublin, Dubai, Riyadh and Beirut, is at the forefront of cloud technology and digital transformation. Our mission is to empower businesses by delivering top-tier cloud solutions. As we continue to grow and lead in innovation, we are looking to enrich our team with a Senior AWS DevOps Engineer who is passionate about automation, cloud architecture, and continuous improvement.
 Role Overview:
 We’re seeking a seasoned Senior AWS DevOps Engineer with a minimum of 5 years of experience, including team management and involvement in a Cloud Center of Excellence (CCoE). This role involves leading our DevOps initiatives, driving automation, improving system performance, and mentoring a team of talented engineers. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in AWS services, infrastructure as code, and a proven track record of implementing DevOps best practices in a cloud environment.
 Key Responsibilities:
 Lead and manage the DevOps team, setting goals and developing career paths for team members.
 Spearhead the design and implementation of fully automated CI/CD pipelines for code deployment across multiple environments.
 Actively contribute to the development and maintenance of the Cloud Center of Excellence, sharing best practices, and driving cloud adoption across the organization.
 Oversee and optimize infrastructure as code (IaC) using tools such as Terraform, CloudFormation, or similar.
 Ensure system reliability, scalability, and security through effective cloud architecture and proactive monitoring.
 Drive incident management processes and contribute to root cause analysis and resolution.
 Foster a culture of continuous improvement by introducing new technologies and processes that enhance operational efficiency.
 Requirements:
 At least 5 years of experience in DevOps, with a strong focus on AWS cloud services.
 Demonstrated experience managing DevOps teams and contributing to a Cloud Center of Excellence.
 Proficiency in CI/CD tooling (e.g., Jenkins, GitLab CI, CircleCI) and IaC (e.g., Terraform, AWS CloudFormation).
 Expertise in scripting languages (e.g., Python, Bash) and configuration management tools (e.g., Ansible, Chef, Puppet).
 Strong understanding of containerization and orchestration technologies (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes).
 Familiarity with monitoring and logging tools (e.g., CloudWatch, ELK stack, Prometheus).
 Excellent problem-solving, teamwork, and communication skills.
 Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent professional experience.
 What We Offer:
 The opportunity to lead and shape the future of DevOps within a leading cloud solutions provider.
 A collaborative, inclusive, and dynamic work environment that encourages innovation and continuous learning.
 Competitive compensation, comprehensive insurance benefits, bonus and opportunities for professional growth and development.
